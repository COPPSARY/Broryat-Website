Broryat can help a Telegram group review high-risk messages without forcing members to leave the chat to inspect content elsewhere.

## Steps

1. Open the `@broryat_bot` profile
2. Tap `Add to Group or Channel`
3. Choose a group you manage
4. Send a test message or forward one suspicious item

## Before you use it

Let members know the bot is for safety reviews, not automatic moderation in this version.
