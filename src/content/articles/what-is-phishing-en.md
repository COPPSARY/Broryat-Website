Phishing is a scam technique that uses messages or pages that look official to trick people into entering passwords, OTP codes, or banking details. The message usually creates urgency with language like "your account will be locked" or "confirm this payment now."

## Key signs

- The URL does not match the real organization
- The message pushes for immediate action
- The sender asks for OTP codes or passwords
- The page uses a familiar logo but a different domain

## What to do

Do not click directly from a suspicious message. Send the link to `@broryat_bot` first. If the message claims to come from a bank or service, open the official app or website yourself instead of following the link.
